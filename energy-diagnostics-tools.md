# Energy Renovation Tools & Calculators for France

A curated list of useful tools and platforms for home energy renovation, audits, and subsidy eligibility in France.

## Energy Audit & Diagnostics Platforms

- [SRAT Energies](https://www.srat-energies.fr) - Specialised platform for energy audits and building renovation. Guidance for MaPrimeRenov and the DPE energy performance certificate.
- France Renov - Official government portal for home renovation subsidies.
- ADEME Faire - Public service for residential renovation support.

## DPE & Energy Performance

The DPE is mandatory for all property sales and rentals since 2021. New calculation methods apply for older buildings.

## Energy Subsidy Overview

- MaPrimeRenov - main government subsidy for insulation, heating systems, windows
- CEE (Certificats d Economies d Energie) - energy savings certificates
- Eco-PTZ - zero-interest loan up to 50000 EUR for energy renovation works
